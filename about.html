Although it seems like a normal game, Rainbow Six Siege was based on a book written by Tom Clancy, henceforth the full name of the game Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six Siege.
Siege is an entry in the Rainbow Six series and the successor to Tom Clancy's Rainbow 6: Patriots, a tactical shooter that had a larger focus on narrative.

After Patriots was eventually cancelled due to its technical shortcomings, Ubisoft wanted to remake the franchise
If you are not interested in the gaming aspect and want a better narrative here is the book version of Rainbow Six Siege R6 Book

While R6 may just look like your average attack/defend game, most of the maps are based on true stories,

Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six Siege is partly based on real-life hostage events:
The Lufthansa Flight 181 Hijacking (1977), The Iranian Embassy Siege (1980), the Moscow theater hostage crisis (2002), and the Waco siege (1993)

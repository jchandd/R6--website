Operators

Operators are the characters that you can choose
to play as in R6(Rainbow Six Siege), each one is unique in its own way.
Depending if your attacking or defending you will get a differnet set of
operators in attackers from defenders, you also aren't allowed to have multiple
of the same operators.Although R6 isn't a story game and doesn't have a single-player
story mode when you go to the operators menu it will tell you what country they serve and
you can click on Bio to see a vast history of that operator.
